Foods That Benefit Your Health

  • Fruits & Vegetables: Packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber for overall health.
  • Whole Grains: Oats, brown rice, quinoa, and whole wheat for lasting energy and heart health.
  • Lean Proteins: Chicken, fish, beans, tofu, and eggs for muscle and tissue repair.
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, olive oil, nuts, and seeds for brain and heart support.
  • Low-Fat Dairy: Milk, yogurt, and cheese for strong bones.
  • Water: Keeps you hydrated and supports all body functions.

Foods That Are Not Beneficial

  • Sugary Drinks: Soda, energy drinks, and sweetened teas add empty calories.
  • Processed Snacks: Chips, cookies, and packaged pastries are high in unhealthy fats, sugar, and salt.
  • Fried Foods: French fries, fried chicken, and similar foods are high in unhealthy fats and calories.
  • Refined Grains: White bread, white rice, and regular pasta have less fiber and nutrients.
  • Excess Salt: Too much salt can raise blood pressure and harm your heart.
  • Processed Meats: Sausages, hot dogs, and deli meats often contain unhealthy fats and preservatives.

Eat at regular times, choose whole foods, and enjoy a colorful plate for the best nutrition every day!
