Skin Care

Simple steps for healthy, glowing skin

Cleanse

Wash Your Face

Wash your face every morning and night with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil.

Moisturize

Use Moisturizer

Apply moisturizer after washing your face to keep your skin soft and prevent dryness.

Protect

Wear Sunscreen

Use sunscreen every day, even when it’s cloudy, to protect your skin from sun damage.

Eat Well

Healthy Foods

Eat fruits, vegetables, and healthy fats. Good food helps your skin look and feel better.

Hydrate

Drink Water

Drink enough water every day. Hydrated skin looks fresh and healthy.

Exfoliate

Remove Dead Skin

Gently scrub your skin once a week to remove dead skin cells and help your skin glow.

Rest

Get Enough Sleep

Try to sleep 7-9 hours each night. Good sleep helps your skin repair and look bright.

Prevent

Don’t Touch Your Face

Keep your hands away from your face to avoid spreading germs and causing breakouts.

Hygiene

Change Pillowcase

Change your pillowcase often. Clean bedding helps keep your skin clear.

Prevent

Don’t Pick Pimples

Don’t squeeze or pick at pimples. This can cause scars and more breakouts.

Advice

See a Skin Doctor

If you have skin problems that don’t go away, talk to a dermatologist for help.
