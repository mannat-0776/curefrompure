Mental Health Resources

Stress Management

Daily stress relief techniques

Stress Management

  • Practice deep breathing and progressive muscle relaxation
  • Use time management tools to prioritize tasks
  • Take regular breaks and engage in hobbies
  • Try journaling to process emotions

Anxiety Relief

Natural anxiety reduction

Anxiety Relief

  • Practice mindfulness and grounding exercises
  • Limit caffeine and sugar intake
  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule
  • Connect with supportive friends or groups

Depression Support

Resources for mood support

Depression Support

  • Establish a daily routine and set small goals
  • Engage in physical activity and sunlight exposure
  • Reach out to mental health professionals
  • Join support groups or talk to trusted people

Mindfulness & Meditation

Calm your mind and body

Mindfulness & Meditation

  • Try guided meditation apps or videos
  • Practice mindful breathing and body scans
  • Incorporate short mindfulness breaks into your day
  • Use gratitude journaling to stay present

Sleep Health

Tips for restful sleep

Sleep Health

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Keep your sleep environment cool and dark

Self-Esteem & Confidence

Build a positive self-image

Self-Esteem & Confidence

  • Practice positive self-talk and affirmations
  • Set achievable goals and celebrate progress
  • Surround yourself with supportive people
  • Reflect on your strengths and achievements

Addiction Recovery

Support for overcoming addiction

Addiction Recovery

  • Access professional counseling and therapy
  • Join peer support groups (e.g., AA, NA)
  • Develop healthy coping strategies
  • Build a strong support network

Nutrition & Mental Health

Eat well for your mind

Nutrition & Mental Health

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Stay hydrated and limit processed foods
  • Include omega-3 fatty acids for brain health
  • Maintain regular meal times

Social Connection

Build supportive relationships

Social Connection

  • Reach out to friends and family regularly
  • Participate in community or group activities
  • Volunteer or join clubs with shared interests
  • Practice active listening and empathy

Work-Life Balance

Find harmony in life

Work-Life Balance

  • Set boundaries between work and personal time
  • Schedule regular breaks and vacations
  • Prioritize self-care and hobbies
  • Communicate needs with employers and family

Grief & Loss Support

Help through difficult times

Grief & Loss Support

  • Allow yourself to grieve and express emotions
  • Seek support from friends, family, or counselors
  • Join grief support groups
  • Practice self-care and patience with healing

Emotional Intelligence

Understand and manage emotions

Emotional Intelligence

  • Recognize and label your emotions
  • Practice empathy and active listening
  • Develop healthy ways to express feelings
  • Reflect on emotional triggers and responses

Coping with Change

Adapt to life transitions

Coping with Change

  • Accept change as a part of life
  • Focus on what you can control
  • Seek support and talk about your feelings
  • Practice flexibility and resilience skills

Healthy Relationships

Build strong connections

Healthy Relationships

  • Communicate openly and honestly
  • Respect boundaries and differences
  • Show appreciation and support
  • Resolve conflicts constructively

Positive Thinking

Boost your outlook

Positive Thinking

  • Challenge negative thoughts with evidence
  • Practice daily gratitude and optimism
  • Surround yourself with positivity
  • Visualize success and positive outcomes

Time Management

Organize your day

Time Management

  • Use planners or digital calendars
  • Break tasks into manageable steps
  • Set priorities and deadlines
  • Allow time for rest and self-care

Digital Detox

Unplug for mental clarity

Digital Detox

  • Schedule regular screen-free time
  • Engage in offline hobbies and activities
  • Turn off notifications during downtime
  • Practice mindful technology use

Gratitude Practice

Appreciate the good

Gratitude Practice

  • Keep a daily gratitude journal
  • Express thanks to others regularly
  • Reflect on positive experiences
  • Notice small joys in everyday life

Physical Activity

Move for your mind

Physical Activity

  • Engage in regular exercise (walk, run, yoga, etc.)
  • Find activities you enjoy to stay motivated
  • Use movement as a stress reliever
  • Incorporate stretching and breaks into your day
