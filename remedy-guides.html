Turmeric

Anti-inflammatory powerhouse

Health Benefits

  • Reduces inflammation
  • Supports joint health
  • Boosts antioxidant capacity

How to Use

  • Add to curries and soups
  • Mix with warm milk (golden milk)
  • Take as a supplement

Ginger

Digestive aid & nausea relief

Health Benefits

  • Relieves nausea
  • Reduces muscle pain
  • Improves digestion

How to Use

  • Brew as tea
  • Add to stir-fries and smoothies
  • Chew raw or take capsules

Honey

Natural cough suppressant

Health Benefits

  • Soothes sore throat
  • Antibacterial properties
  • Boosts energy

How to Use

  • Add to tea or warm water
  • Use as a natural sweetener
  • Apply to minor wounds

Garlic

Immune system booster

Health Benefits

  • Supports immune function
  • Lowers blood pressure
  • Antimicrobial effects

How to Use

  • Eat raw or cooked in meals
  • Add to sauces and dressings
  • Take as a supplement

Chamomile

Health Benefits

  • Promotes relaxation and sleep
  • Reduces anxiety and stress
  • Helps with digestive issues
  • Anti-inflammatory properties

How to Use

  • Chamomile tea before bedtime
  • Essential oil in diffuser
  • Topical application for skin irritation

Precautions

May cause allergic reactions in people sensitive to ragweed.

Eucalyptus

Health Benefits

  • Relieves respiratory congestion
  • Natural decongestant
  • Pain relief for sore muscles
  • Insect repellent properties

How to Use

  • Steam inhalation with essential oil
  • Topical rub for muscle pain
  • Diffuser for air purification

Precautions

Not safe for internal use. Keep away from children and pets.

Ashwagandha

Health Benefits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • May boost brain function
  • Can lower blood sugar levels
  • May increase muscle strength

How to Use

  • Powder in warm milk or water
  • Capsules as supplement
  • Added to smoothies

Precautions

May interact with thyroid medications. Avoid during pregnancy.

Neem

Health Benefits

  • Powerful antibacterial properties
  • Helps with skin conditions
  • Supports oral health
  • May help regulate blood sugar

How to Use

  • Neem oil for skin applications
  • Neem twigs for oral hygiene
  • Neem leaf tea

Precautions

Bitter taste. Not recommended for long-term internal use.

Cinnamon

Health Benefits

  • May lower blood sugar levels
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • May reduce heart disease risk

How to Use

  • Sprinkle on oatmeal or yogurt
  • Cinnamon tea
  • Added to smoothies

Precautions

Cassia cinnamon contains coumarin which may be harmful in large doses.
